Who we are?



The history of BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke AG) is a fascinating journey that dates back to the early 20th century. Here's a summary of the key milestones in the history of this iconic automotive brand: Foundation and Early Years (1916-1930): BMW was founded on March 7, 1916, as Bayerische Flugzeugwerke AG (BFw) in Munich, Germany, initially as an aircraft engine manufacturer. After World War I, the company shifted its focus to the production of engines for motorcycles and automobiles.
During World War II, BMW turned to the production of aircraft engines and military vehicles. Post-War Reconstruction and Revival (1945-1960): After the war, BMW's factory lay in ruins, but the company reorganized and resumed production of motorcycles and automobiles in the 1950s.

BMW has remained a leading luxury car manufacturer and introduced several popular models, including the BMW X5 (its first SUV) and the BMW i3 (an electric vehicle). BMW has become one of the world's most recognized and respected automobile brands, known for its focus on performance, quality, and design


Its history is filled with remarkable achievements and will continue to evolve as the automotive industry moves toward new technologies and challenges.
